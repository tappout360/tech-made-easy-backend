# Technical Made Easy — Integration Ecosystem

### 15 Powerful Integrations That Transform Your Business

> *Every integration below is plug-and-play from your Company Dashboard → Integrations tab. No coding required.*

## 💰 Financial & Accounting

### QuickBooks
**What it does:** Auto-syncs invoices, purchase orders, and billing data directly to your QuickBooks account. Work orders generate line items, labor hours, and parts costs without manual re-entry.

**Performance impact:** Eliminates 5-10 hours/week of double data entry. Reduces billing errors by 90%. Invoices go out same-day instead of end-of-week.

---

### Prophet 21 (P21)
**What it does:** Connects to Epicor Prophet 21 ERP for real-time parts pricing, inventory levels, and purchase order creation. Techs see live stock counts while writing work orders.

**Performance impact:** Prevents ordering parts you already have in stock. Cuts parts procurement time by 60%. Real-time pricing means accurate quotes on the spot.

---

### Salesforce
**What it does:** Pushes new client accounts, completed work orders, and service history into your Salesforce CRM. Sales teams see equipment service data alongside client accounts.

**Performance impact:** Gives your sales team full visibility into service relationships. Upsell PM contracts to clients with aging equipment. Close 25% more renewals with data-driven conversations.

---

### SAP
**What it does:** Enterprise-grade ERP integration for large biomed companies. Syncs asset records, maintenance schedules, and cost center allocation with SAP modules.

**Performance impact:** Single source of truth for equipment lifecycle. Eliminates reconciliation between field service and back-office. Supports audit requirements for ISO 13485 environments.

---

## 📅 Scheduling & Productivity

### Google Calendar
**What it does:** Auto-creates calendar events for scheduled PMs, dispatched work orders, and follow-up appointments. Two-way sync means changes in either place stay updated.

**Performance impact:** Techs never miss a scheduled PM or follow-up. Office staff see real-time tech availability. Reduces no-shows and scheduling conflicts by 80%.

---

### Google Maps
**What it does:** Powers the "Map My Day" route planner. Optimizes multi-stop routes for technicians, calculates drive times, and generates turn-by-turn directions. Detects when teammates have WOs nearby.

**Performance impact:** Saves 45-90 minutes of drive time per tech per day. Reduces fuel costs 15-25%. Techs complete 1-2 more work orders daily with optimized routing.

---

### Zoom
**What it does:** Launch video calls directly from a work order for remote diagnostics. Senior techs can guide junior techs through complex repairs via live video without driving to the site.

**Performance impact:** Resolves 20-30% of equipment issues remotely — no truck roll needed. Cuts average resolution time for complex repairs. Accelerates training for new technicians.

---

## 💬 Team Communication

### Slack
**What it does:** Sends real-time notifications to Slack channels when work orders are created, completed, or flagged as emergencies. Techs and office staff stay synced without checking the app.

**Performance impact:** Emergency WOs get seen in <60 seconds. Reduces "did you see the new WO?" phone calls by 90%. Creates a searchable history of all service communications.

---

### Microsoft Teams
**What it does:** Same as Slack integration but for Microsoft 365 environments. WO alerts, PM reminders, and dispatch notifications flow directly into Teams channels.

**Performance impact:** Keeps your team informed without leaving their primary workspace. Perfect for enterprise environments that standardize on Microsoft 365.

---

## 📄 Documents & Compliance

### DocuSign
**What it does:** Sends PM contracts, service agreements, and client approvals through DocuSign for legally binding e-signatures. Signed documents auto-attach to the client record.

**Performance impact:** Contracts get signed 5x faster than paper/email. Full audit trail for HIPAA and regulatory compliance. Eliminates lost paperwork and unsigned agreements.

---

### PDF Reports
**What it does:** Auto-generates professional PDF reports for completed work orders, PM inspections, compliance audits, and billing summaries. Branded with your company logo and colors.

**Performance impact:** Deliver polished, branded reports to clients instantly. Meet TJC/CMS documentation requirements. Archive every service record as a compliant PDF.

---

## ⚡ Automation & Workflow

### Webhooks
**What it does:** Send real-time HTTP notifications to any external system when events occur — WO created, WO completed, PM due, emergency flagged. Build custom automations with any platform.

**Performance impact:** Connect to ANY system that accepts webhooks. Trigger custom workflows (update a database, send an SMS, push to a dashboard). Unlimited automation potential.

---

### IFTTT
**What it does:** "If This Then That" — create simple automations like "If an emergency WO is created, text the on-call manager" or "If a PM is overdue, email the client."

**Performance impact:** Non-technical staff can create automations in minutes. Automate repetitive notifications and follow-ups. 50+ trigger/action combinations available.

---

### Power Automate
**What it does:** Microsoft's enterprise automation platform. Build complex workflows: auto-generate reports in SharePoint, create tickets in ServiceDesk, sync data to Excel Online.

**Performance impact:** Enterprise-grade automation for Microsoft shops. Connect to 400+ Microsoft connectors. Automate cross-platform workflows with drag-and-drop logic.

---

### ServiceNow
**What it does:** Pushes work orders and asset data into ServiceNow ITSM for organizations that use ServiceNow as their central service management platform.

**Performance impact:** Eliminates dual ticketing systems. Biomed work orders flow alongside IT tickets in one unified platform. Supports ITIL-aligned processes.
